Who We Are

Built on intelligent design,
grounded in real craft.

Dez-Intell Workstation is an Accra-based architecture and build studio. We work with families, homeowners and businesses who want a space that's been thought through — not just thrown together — and we stay involved from the first sketch to the final coat of paint.

Our name reflects how we work: design paired with intelligence. Every project starts with listening — to your budget, your land, your lifestyle — before a single line is drawn. That's how we keep ambitious ideas practical, and practical projects beautiful.

Client-First

Your goals and budget shape every decision, from concept to completion.

Hands-On

We oversee build and renovation work directly — no vanishing after the drawings.

Locally Rooted

We understand Accra's climate, materials and terrain, and design accordingly.

Detail-Driven

From floor plans to landscaping, the small choices get the same care as the big ones.

What We Do

Everything your project needs — under one roof.

Whether you're starting from an empty plot or reimagining a space you already own, we cover the full journey — so you're not coordinating between several different contractors.

Architectural Design

Concept drawings, floor plans and 3D visualization that turn your ideas into a buildable, permit-ready design.

Construction & Building

From groundbreaking to handover, we manage the build with skilled tradespeople and steady site supervision.

Remodeling & Renovation

Outgrown your space without wanting to move? We restructure, extend and modernize homes and offices with minimal disruption.

Interior Design

Layouts, finishes, lighting and furnishing plans that make a finished structure feel like home — or like business.

Landscape Design

Gardens, compounds and outdoor living areas designed to suit the climate and complement the architecture.

Project Management & Consultancy

Budgeting, timelines, permits and contractor coordination — handled or advised on, so your project stays on track from day one.

Site Supervision & Quality Inspection

Regular site visits and quality checks throughout the build, so issues are caught early — not after the concrete sets.
